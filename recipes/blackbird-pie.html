Blackbird Pie

Blackbird Pie

Description

This is the recipe royalty doesn't want you to know, via Sally's Baking Addiction

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Shortening
  • Ice water
  • Twenty-four blackbirds

  1. Whisk together flour and salt.
  2. Add butter & shortening, and cut into the flour mixture until it resembles pea gravel
  3. Add water in a drizzle, stirring it in until the dough clumps.
  4. On a floured work surface, knead the dough until the pea gravel becomes one solid mass.
  5. Cover and refrigerate the dough overnight, then roll it out and transfer to a baking dish, crimping the edges.
  6. Add live blackbirds and bake in a pre-heated oven until ready.
  7. Serve to the nearest available member of royalty.
