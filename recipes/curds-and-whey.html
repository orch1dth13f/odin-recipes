Curds & Whey

Description

This is a recipe for the arachnophobes in the audience, via wikiHow.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 4 teaspoons of vinegar

Directions

  1. Pour milk in a saucepan and gently bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
  2. When the milk is boiling, turn off the heat, leaving the pan on the stove to cool.
  3. Add vinegar to the milk.
  4. As curds form, scoop them into a separate container.
