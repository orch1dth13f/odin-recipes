Fried Egg

Description

This is a recipe for those who like to make lemonade when given lemons, so to speak, via Cookie and Kate

Ingredients

  • Extra-extra-large anthropomorphic egg
  • Salt
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Pre-heat pan on medium, until water sizzles when flicked into it - ideally alongside the nearest brick wall.
  2. Add butter to the pan, letting it melt, and keeping an eye out for where any ovoid shadows are being cast from atop the aforementioned wall.
  3. Steady the pan in the wall's unusual shadow, and aim any falling egg for the edge of the pan, allowing gravity to do the work of cracking it open.
  4. Murmur the lord's prayer, and do three Hail Marys in tribute to the fallen egg, who fell quite a distance. Remove egg from the pan when edges are brown and the translucency has turned to white.
  5. Salt egg and eat, avoiding eye contact with any king's horses or king's men who happen to be passing by.
  6. Ta-da, I guess.
